What is mocha coffee?

Mocha coffee is a staple popular for its unique flavor and sweetness. It is commonly sold in cafes and grocery stores.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hot brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. Pour hot coffee into a mug.
  2. Stir in cocoa, sugar, and milk.
