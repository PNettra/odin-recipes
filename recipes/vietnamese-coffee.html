What is Vietnamese coffee?

Both strong and tasty, Vietnamese coffee is a popular staple in Asian stores, restaurants, and cafes worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • ½ cup dark roast ground coffee beans
  • ½ cup sweetened condensed milk, divided
  • 16 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Brew water with coffee using your preferred method to make coffee.
  2. Spoon 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk into each of 4 coffee cups.
  3. Pour 1 cup fresh hot coffee into each cup and stir to dissolve the milk.
  4. Serve cups of coffee along with 4 tall glasses filled with 4 ice cubes each and a long-handled spoon.
  5. Pour hot coffee over ice cubes and stir briskly with the long-handled spoon to chill the coffee.
